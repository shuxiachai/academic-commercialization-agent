## Reports

### [Perovskite Solar Cells for Building-Integrated Photovoltaics](perovskite-solar-cells-bipv.md)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Topic** | Perovskite solar cells for building-integrated photovoltaics (BIPV) |
| **Technology stage** | Late research / early prototype |
| **Key market** | BIPV market: $24.1B (2023) → $125B projected (2032) |
| **Sources** | 2 academic papers · 5 patents · 7 market reports |

**Headline finding:** Lab efficiencies reach 25.24% with new air-stable UV absorbers,
but long-term outdoor validation data (currently ≤40 days) falls far short of the
20-year lifespan BIPV requires. Active patent filings from Chinese firms and ENI SPA
create both opportunity and freedom-to-operate risk for new entrants.

### [CAR-T Cell Therapy for Solid Tumors](car-t-therapy-solid-tumors.md)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Topic** | CAR-T cell therapy targeting solid tumors |
| **Technology stage** | Pre-commercial / proof-of-concept |
| **Key market** | Overall CAR-T market: $5.2–9B (2025); no approved solid-tumor product as of 2025 |
| **Sources** | 3 academic papers · 8 patents · 8 market reports |

**Headline finding:** Remarkable success in blood cancers has not translated to solid
tumors due to the immunosuppressive tumour microenvironment and antigen escape.
This creates a high-risk, high-opportunity first-mover gap, with promising approaches
(pluripotent stem cell-derived CAR-T, multi-targeting, CAR-ligand vaccines) still
in preclinical stages.

### [Solid-State Batteries for Electric Vehicles](solid-state-batteries-ev.md)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Topic** | Solid-state batteries for electric vehicle applications |
| **Technology stage** | Pre-industrial prototype; commercial entry expected 2027–2030 |
| **Key market** | Solid-state battery market: $119M–$1.6B (2025, high uncertainty) |
| **Sources** | 4 academic papers · 8 patents · 7 market reports |

**Headline finding:** Ionic conductivities now rival liquid electrolytes (>10⁻² S/cm)
and prototypes exceed 500 Wh/kg with >80% retention after 800 cycles, but scalable
manufacturing remains the primary bottleneck. Toyota, Samsung SDI, CATL, and
QuantumScape are all active; patent filings focus on thermal management and pack
integration rather than electrolyte chemistry — a white space worth watching.

## How these were generated

Each report was produced by running the agent with a free-text research topic and
no paper seed:

```
Topic: perovskite solar cells for building-integrated photovoltaics
Paper seed URL: (left blank)
```

Total wall-clock time per report: approximately 8–12 minutes.
LLM backend: GPT-4o-mini (source pipeline) + GPT-4o (analysis agents).
